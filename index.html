Process

  1. Learn & Define

    Observe, learn and frame the problem to solve, the opportunity. Empathise with the customer (e.g: analytics, JTBD, experience maps, customer journeys, interviews, affinity mapping, personas). Define success metrics. Take some risks, make some bets.

  2. Generate ideas

    Explore ideas based on what we learned (e.g: sketching, rapid prototyping, sprints, workshops).

  3. Test ideas

    Test the ideas, measure and validate (e.g: usability testing, interviews, surveys, customer feedback, data analytics, sprints).

  4. Make them perfect

    Stick with the ideas that worked according to your metrics. Release and iterate, measure again.

Goals

    There's enough bad design around and I want to fight it. I want to help to build products that solve real problems and make people's lives easier.
