Let me tell you a story...

2018-2019, London.

What's HeadBox?

HeadBox is an events agency with a digital marketplace that connects people organising events with venues. The proposition is quite simple, guests send briefs to venues and venues respond them. Our agents provide a concierge service to our corporate accounts.

HeadBox before the new product team joined

  • Data is all over the place and hard to make sense of it
  • The product was designed in isolation of customer insights.
  • A Head of Product and a freelance designer designed
    • the whole product in a silo to then give a long list of specifications to a remote developement agency to deliver code.
  • This resulted in a marketplace with a 1% conversion rate, a funnel leaking 90% of their users before their first call to action, and a failed CRM that no one wants to use. Head of Product, designer and one PM were fired :(
  • A business model based on commission does not help any of the parties involved

The first months for the team

  • Organised workshops to gather internal internal and external knowledge about the industry.
  • Gathered insights around customer's and non customer's roles, needs, problems and current processes to identify opportunities
  • Created personas based on insights
  • Organised 3 rounds of Interviews with 45 customers and non customers
  • Observed users going through the current flow
  • Organised current data sources and generated new ones to understand customer behaviour and problems in the current experience.
  • Educated about a customer centric approach to the entire company by inviting them to interviews and design studios
  • Mapped current user flows and architecture
    • Current HeadBox guest and host flows
  • Automated manual processes like invoicing and payments
    • Current HeadBox payments flows
  • Fixed bugs and mantained the current experience, while launching the platform internationally
  • Launched 2 new products that were already ready to be released
  • Commanded the customer support chat to gather feedback directly from the customers
  • Analysed the content and relationships of thousands of messages between customers and briefs sent to HeadBox
  • Analysed competitors UX to benchmark our offering
  • Analysed the most common problems sent to support emails
  • Decided on a strategy on how to create UI with reusable components

Communicating insights

HeadBox team composition is mostly 90% sales with an average age of 23 years old. In the beginning we struggled to get people interested in our insights..

Things that worked:

A weekly email with bad news based in data insights but with a joyful tone


Inviting everyone to customer interviews and workshops

Research synthesis in funny videos


All this helped creating a shared understading about the big problems we had to tackle, to priortise and educate on how products can we built using insights from data and customer interviews

The big problems identified

After analysing opportunities and prioritising, we decided to tackle the following problems

  • Venues not being able to manage their own listings
  • Guests not being able to find a venue and sending briefs to a support email
  • Guests don't really know what they want and they are overwhelwed by the amount of options
  • Multiple opportunities in the journey to cut out the marketplace
  • The effort put into converting small meeting rooms causes losses
  • Venues wanting more events from us but at the same time not wanting to be spammed with irrelevant events
  • A 1% conversion rate and and drop of 90% in the funnel before the main call to action

The new HeadBox

The new HeadBox will allow guests to send their brief as detailed as they need but not more than that, will allow venues to easily list and find new business without spam

  • A new way of sending briefs (prototype). Final version will be launched next month
  • A new way of listing a venue (prototype). Final version will be launched next month
  • A new way for venues to get more events without the spam. It's live in Beta
  • Integration and partnerships with meeting room providers. Planned for Q4
  • HeadBox health monitoring

    • With a new tracking system we will be able to learn from our customer's usage and use this data to suggest the better matches for events.

    With a new Admin panel account managers will be able to oversee all activity
